2.Our Curltural Events - "Mridang"

Some stunned by reciting the song, while some forced others to dance with a scintillating dance. At the same time, the tunes of the band filled the enthusiasm among the students. The fashion show in the evening made the atmosphere more pleasant. The occasion was on Friday to organize Mridang-2020, the annual festival of Meerut Institute of Technology-MIT located at Partapur Bypass.
Mridang started with Ganesh Puja, Saraswati Vandana and presentation of folk dances from different states. The students depicted the culture by presenting folk dances of the states of Arunachal Pradesh, Meghalaya, Punjab, Haryana, Rajasthan, West Bengal, Uttar Pradesh etc. in a program based on the theme of Bharat Shreshtha Bharat. In Mridang, the students enthralled the audience with new and old songs of Bollywood and Hollywood in singing competition Awaaz A Virasat.In the afternoon, three teams participated in the Battle of Bands competition. More than one performance of Bollywood songs made the cultural evening of Mridang melodious. On the band, the students presented the message of humanity of Sufi saints like Kabir and Baba Farid etc. In the evening, the Neeve Band performance gave a scintillating performance on Bollywood songs. The highlight of the event was the fashion show The Mirage in the evening. The fashion show gave a social themed message by wearing exotic outfits with Indo Western, Goanese Bollywood Wedding. The ramp walk enthralled everyone. At the same time, the students danced a lot on DJ Night.
The one-day youth festival was inaugurated by lighting the lamp jointly by Chairman Vishnu Sharan, Vice Chairman Puneet Agarwal, Director Dr. Alok Chauhan, Principal Dr. Himanshu Sharma, Coordinator Neeraj Kant Sharma, Swati Sharma. BPharma Principal Dr. Neeraj Kant Sharma, KN Tripathi, Suraj Malik, Suresh Hans, Mohammad Iqbal, Ajay Chaudhary etc. were present on this occasion.
